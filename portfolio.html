Byeol

Byeol is a Korean word that means Star! Byeol is an ecommerce shop name for our project in Web Development. I created the Logo as well as the shop name, which means that each item the shop offers is a star in its own right, waiting to be discovered by its buyer!

COMET.AL

COMET.AL is also an ecommerce shop name that I made for our project in Web Development. The shop name means Come Et Al. which means Come Others! The Comet was a birth of the combination, which I also gave meaning too: Like comets leaving a trail of wonder, each item in our shop is carefully selected to leave a lasting impression.

What If?

What If? is a Manga/Manhwa style artwork i created for our project in Reading Visual Arts. Manga are comics or graphic novels originating from Japan, as someone who loves reading manga, I had the urge to make one for the project, and I got a perfect score for it. It was really hard to make, and a lot of efforts was put into it.

Synopsis:

Asakura Jo is a typical highschooler, bland, boring, lame, a potato? Then theres Yoshino Eino, the star of the class, a knight in shining armor. Jo liked Eino, and what if Eino also like Jo? What if they're just waiting for one another? What if the wait takes too long? What if...?
